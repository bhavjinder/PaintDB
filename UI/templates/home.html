Overview

PaIntDB contains information on protein interactions found in Pseudomonas aeruginosa.
  • Strains currently supported are PAO1 and PA14.
  • Protein interactions are defined as:
    • protein-protein,
    • protein-metabolite, or
    • transcription factor-binding site.
