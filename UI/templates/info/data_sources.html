Data Sources

PaIntDB is built with data accumulated from multiple source databases. Interactions in PaIntDB were either found in the specified P. aeruginosa strain, or were obtained through mapping of orthologous interactions from the other P. aeruginosa strain or Escherichia coli K12 substr. MG1655.
